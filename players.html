PV Sindhu

Sindhu has been employed with Bharat Petroleum since July 2013, as an assistant sports manager with their Hyderabad office. Following her silver-medal win at the Rio Olympics, she was promoted to deputy sports manager. She was appointed as the first brand ambassador of Bridgestone India.[163] She was appointed as the Deputy Collector (Group-I) by the Andhra Pradesh government in July 2017, which she took charge later in August.[164] She was the flag bearer for the Indian contingent in the opening ceremony of the 2018 and 2022 Commonwealth Games

Parupalli Kashyap

Kashyap first started playing badminton recreationally in 1997. He was just an 11-year-old child then and tried his hand at badminton during a summer camp. But the hobby soon turned into a passion and eventually a dream! Kashyap took first baby steps into the badminton world with the guidance of Dronacharya awardee and Padma Shri S.M. Arif. Later, he moved to Bangalore and trained at the Prakash Padukone Academy for three years.
